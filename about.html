About Cube Earth Theory

I made Cube Earth Theory to criticize and spread light towards conspiracy theories and the very real harm that they can bring towards people of all backgrounds. The content on this site is primarily meant to entertain and not to be taken seriously, but the truth is that there are many websites, social media accounts, and real-world organizations that spread real world misinformation and bring people into a self-destructive cycle of conspiratorial thinking. This type of thinking does not just involve believing quirky pseudoscience, but it almost always promotes real world discrimination and hatred. Even seemingly harmless groups like Flat Earth, the main inspiration for this site, have beliefs rooted in antisemetic conspiracy. I hope that this website can help to combat these harmful movements by exposing the absurdity of their argumentation. If you wish to learn further, the article Are Conspiracy Theories Harmless? from cambridge.org goes into greater detail the dangers brought forth by conspiracy theories.
